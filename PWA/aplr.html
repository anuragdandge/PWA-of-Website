Number System

20/04/2021


1) Natural Number :-
  Natural Numbers are counting numbers Starts from 1,2,3... are the most familiar numbers. They are involved symbolically represent by "N" , sum of two natural number is natural number . The product of two natural is natural number.

2) Whole Number :-
   Natural number including with 0 (zero) is known as Whole Number .

3) Integer :-
   Natural number are subset of integers. Natural number without zero are called Integer .

4) Rational Number :-
   A fraction with an integer numerator & positive integer in the denominator it is represented in the form of p/q where , 'p' is integer and 'q' is non-zero integer or is not equal to zero .
An integer are rational number . Decimal number which have repeating pattern will also fall under this category .
eg. 0.83333 = 1/12

5) Irrational Number :-
   Irrational number that cannot be written as fraction i.e. p/q .
It is never ending number & does not repeat itself in the decimal form .
eg. Ï€ = 3.14159...

6) Real Number :-
   The set of Rational & Irrational number are called Real Number . Real Number represented by Symbol "R".

7) Prime Number :-
   Any Real Number that is divisible by 1 & itself .
eg. 2,3,5,7,9,11,13,17...

8) Even Number :-
   Natural number which is divisible by only 2 , are Even Number.
eg. 2,4,6,8...

9) Odd Number
   Natural number which is not divisible by 2 , are called Odd Number

Test of Divisibility

26/04/2021


1) Divisibility of 2 :-

 The Number of Divisible by 2 if it's unit digit (last digit) is divisible by any of " 0 , 2 , 4 , 6 , 8 " .

  Ex.   58694  is divisible by 2 .
         86945  is not divisible by 2.


2) Divisibility by 3 :-

  The number of divisibility by 3 only when the sum of its all digit is divisible by 3 .

Ex.   695421 :-
       Sum of all digit = 6+9+5+4+2+1 = 27 ;
       Hence, 695421 is divisible by 3.

Ex.   948653 :-
       Sum of all digit = 9+4+8+6+5+3 = 35 ;
       Hence, 948653 is not divisible by 3.


3) Divisibility by 9 :-

  A number is divisible by 9 when the sum of it's all digit is divisible by 9 .

Ex.  246591 :-
       Sum of all digit = 2+4+6+9+5+1 = 27 ,
       which is divisible by 9 ,
       Hence , 246591 is divisible by 9 .


Ex.  734519 :-
       Sum of all digit = 7+3+4+5+1+9 = 29 ,
       which is not divisible by 9 ,
       Hence , 734519 is not divisible by 9 .

4) Divisibility by 4 :-

  A number is divisible by 4 when if it's last 2 digit is divisible is 4 .

Ex.  6879376:-
       last 2 digit 6879376 = 76/4 = 19 , remainder = 0 ,
       Hence , 6879376 is divisible by 4 .

Ex.  496138:-
       last 2 digit 496138 = 38/4 = 9 , remainder = 2 ,
       Hence , 496138 is not divisible by 4 .


5) Divisibility by 8 :-

  A number is divisible by 8 when if the number formed by hundred's , ten's , unit's digit of the given number is divisible by 8.

Ex.  16789352:-
        16789352 = 352/8= 44 , remainder = 0 ,
       Hence , 16789352 is divisible by 8 .

Ex.  576484:-
        576484 = 484/8 = 60 , remainder = 4 ,
       Hence , 576484 is not divisible by 8 .


6) Divisibility by 10 :-

  A number is divisible by 10 only when it's unit( Last Digit ) digit is 0.
Ex.  657890 :-
   657890 is divisible by 10 .


7) Divisibility by 5 :-

  A number is divisible by 5 only when it's unit( Last Digit ) digit is 0 OR 5.

Ex.  65455 :-
   65455 is divisible by 5 .

 654550 :-
   654550 is divisible by 5 .


8) Divisibility by 11 :-

  A number is divisible by 11 if the difference between the sum of its digit at odd places & sum of it's even places is either zero or divisible by 11 .

(sum of it's odd places digits ) - ( sum of it's even places digits ) = 0 / divisible by 11 .

Ex.   29435417 :-

       ( 7 + 4 + 3 + 9 ) - ( 1 + 5 + 4 + 2 ) =
                 23          -           12           = 11

          11 / 11 = 1 & remainder = 0

       Hence , 29435417 is divisible by 11 .
